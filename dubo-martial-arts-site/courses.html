Our Martial Arts Courses

At DoBu Martial Arts, we offer a variety of courses designed for all skill levels — from beginners to advanced competitors. Our experienced coaches provide expert training in multiple disciplines to help you achieve your goals.

Judo

Learn throws, grappling, and groundwork under the guidance of our 4th Dan Blackbelt coach. Suitable for all ages.

Jiu-Jitsu

Master submission techniques, positional control, and ground fighting with expert instruction from our 3rd Dan Blackbelt.

Karate

Develop striking, kata, and self-discipline training led by our 5th Dan karate coach.

Muay Thai

Train in striking with fists, elbows, knees, and shins with accredited Muay Thai coaches focusing on fitness and technique.

Fitness & Conditioning

Specialist strength and conditioning programs designed for combat athletes, led by qualified fitness coaches.

Self-Defence

Practical self-defence skills for real-life situations taught by our experienced martial arts instructors.
